Executive Summary

  • Best September overall: Napa/Wine Country, Bar Harbor/Acadia, and Reykjavik (cooler weather, lower weather risk than Caribbean).
  • Best quick-and-easy weekend: Puerto Rico (direct options, ~3h flight) and Florida Keys (short flight or drive).
  • Most weather-risky in September: Puerto Rico + Florida Keys (peak Atlantic hurricane season); Costa Rica is rainy (Green Season) but still very doable.
  • Desert West: Great shoulder season transition, but still hot early September (especially Phoenix).

Bar Harbor / Acadia

Top 3 active: Cadillac Mountain sunrise, Jordan Pond & carriage roads biking, Beehive/Precipice hikes (weather dependent).

Top 3 interesting: Acadia NP scenic loop, Bass Harbor Head Light, whale-watch/puffin cruise.

Top 3 eat/drink: Side Street Cafe, Galyn’s, Atlantic Brewing (nearby).

Costa Rica (San José base / transfer)

Top 3 active: Arenal zipline, whitewater rafting (Pacuare), Manuel Antonio hikes/swim.

Top 3 interesting: La Fortuna waterfall + volcano area, Monteverde cloud forest, coffee farm tour.

Top 3 eat/drink: Sikwa, Al Mercat, Apotecario (SJ cocktail bar).

Puerto Rico (San Juan)

Top 3 active: El Yunque hikes, bioluminescent bay kayak (Fajardo/Vieques), surf lesson (Isabela/Condado).

Top 3 interesting: Old San Juan forts, rum distillery tour, day trip to Culebra/Vieques beaches.

Top 3 eat/drink: La Factoría, Santaella, Lote 23.

Desert West (AZ or Albuquerque)

Top 3 active: Sedona hiking, desert ATV/Utv, sunrise hot-air balloon (ABQ).

Top 3 interesting: Grand Canyon South Rim (from PHX), Santa Fe art district (from ABQ), Arizona-Sonora desert museums/landscapes.

Top 3 eat/drink: Pizzeria Bianco (PHX), Geronimo (Santa Fe), Los Poblanos Campo (ABQ).

Napa / Wine Country

Top 3 active: vineyard bike tour, hot-air balloon, kayaking Napa River.

Top 3 interesting: harvest tastings, Castello di Amorosa, Oxbow Public Market.

Top 3 eat/drink: Bistro Don Giovanni, Bouchon (Yountville), Goose & Gander (St. Helena).

Reykjavik / South Coast

Top 3 active: glacier hike, Silfra snorkeling/dive, rugged ATV/lava field tours.

Top 3 interesting: Golden Circle, Blue Lagoon/Sky Lagoon, northern lights hunts (late Sept starts improving).

Top 3 eat/drink: Dill, Food Cellar, MicroBar Reykjavík.

Florida Keys

Top 3 active: reef snorkeling/diving, fishing charter, paddle mangroves (Islamorada/Marathon).

Top 3 interesting: Dry Tortugas day trip, Seven Mile Bridge drive, sunset sail in Key West.

Top 3 eat/drink: Blue Heaven, Latitudes, Santiago’s Bodega.

Assumptions & Notes

  • Sample fares were pulled from public fare aggregators for currently-searchable months as a directional cost baseline.
  • For hurricane-prone destinations (Puerto Rico / Keys), buy refundable or changeable fares and travel insurance.
